# PROBLEM_AND_GOAL

## Problem

Existing platforms fragment knowledge sharing:
- **Map platforms** (Google Maps) have location data but no knowledge feed
- **Content platforms** (Foody, social media) favor images and shallow content, no long-term value
- **Knowledge platforms** (Wikipedia) are reliable but provide no economic incentives for contributors

Result: Users with expertise have no motivation to share deeply, and quality knowledge drowns in entertainment content.

## Target Users

- People who want to share knowledge about places, books, films, topics
- People who "scroll to learn" rather than search
- Users interested in owning and monetizing their knowledge contributions

## Goal

Build a platform where users can:
1. **Write and discover knowledge** via a feed (not just search)
2. **Connect knowledge to real-world context** (places, entities, topics)
3. **Earn reputation and credit** (KS, KNOW-U) through quality contributions
4. **Own long-term contributions** via blockchain (KNOW-G, Solana)

## What the MVP must prove

That users will share quality knowledge, and the community can fairly evaluate, recognize, and reward those contributions — without spam, without manipulation by money.

## Out of scope (MVP)

- DAO governance
- Token trading / swap
- AI-powered features
- Large-scale growth / monetization
